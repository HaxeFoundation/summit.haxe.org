Aleksandr Kuzmenko

Maintainer of the Haxe Compiler

From Russia with love comes Aleksandr, a long-term Haxe user who in 2019 joined the Haxe team in official capicity. He enjoys traveling and has a soft spot for PHP, which lead him to rewrite the Haxe/PHP backend. If something is broken and it isn't Simon's fault, it's probably his.
