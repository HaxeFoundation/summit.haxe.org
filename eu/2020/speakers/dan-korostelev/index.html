Dan Korostelev

Developer at InnoGames and long-time Haxe contributor

Dan, commonly referred to as nadako, has been a long-time contributor to the Haxe compiler. Ever since joining InnoGames in 2018, he has been focused on making the Haxe experience for his colleagues as pleasant as possible. He is also the original author of the vshaxe project.
