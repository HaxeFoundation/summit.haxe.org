Josefiene Pertosa

Office Manager at conlabz, Community Advocate for Haxe

Josefiene has been around in the Haxe community since 2014. Describing herself as "the non-threatening person in the room," she enjoys serving as an interface between the Haxe compiler developer hermits and the real world. She also instigated the organization of the EU Haxe Summit 2020.
