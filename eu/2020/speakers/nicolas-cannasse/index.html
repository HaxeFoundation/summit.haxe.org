Nicolas Cannasse

Director of the Haxe Foundation

Nicolas is the original creator of the Haxe programming language and director of the Haxe Foundation. All he wanted to do was develop games, but that somehow lead to the creation of the entire Haxe ecosystem. Nowadays, he and his fellow developers at Shiro Games use Haxe to create titles such as Evoland, Northgard and Darksburg.
